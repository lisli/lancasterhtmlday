HTML Day 2026
Lancaster, PA

August 8, 2026
1pm-3pm

What is this?

A communal HTML freewrite. A gathering to learn about and write HTML together.

Why?

Why not? HTML is simple and elegant. It is the backbone of the web, and anyone can learn to write and publish it. Most of us interact with it every day.

Who should come?

Anyone who wants to hand write HTML. Those with no experience are welcome and encouraged to attend.

When?

1:30pm-3pm on August 8, 2026

Where?

Buchanan Park, Lancaster city. Specific location within the park will be added closer to the date.

What to bring?

  • Something to write with. Paper, pencil/pen, and/or computer.
  • HTML energy
  • Friends
  • Optionally, a snack to share

Where do I sign up?

Just show up!
